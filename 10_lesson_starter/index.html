About RESTAURANT

TACO RESTAURANT is a family owned business that has been serving the community for over 20 years. We are known for our delicious tacos and friendly service. We offer a variety of tacos, from classic to creative, and we are always looking for new ways to improve our menu. We are located in the heart of the city, and we welcome everyone to come in and try our tacos. We also offer catering services for any event, big or small.

Thank you for choosing TACO RESTAURANT!
